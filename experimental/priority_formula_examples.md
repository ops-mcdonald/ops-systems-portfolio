# Priority Formula Examples

Priority Score = Impact(1-3) + Urgency(1-3) + Risk(0-2)

- 7–8: Critical  
- 5–6: High  
- 3–4: Normal  
- 1–2: Low

Experimented with weighted variations to account for:
- Multiple dependencies
- System area criticality
- Resource availability
